Anidassanasappaṭighe mahābhūte ca āpodhātuñca paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭighaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānaṃ rūpaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ. Phoṭṭhabbāyatanañca āpodhātuñca paṭicca rūpāyatanaṃ. Bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ anidassanasappaṭighe mahābhūte ca āpodhātuñca paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭighaṃ kaṭattārūpaṃ upādārūpaṃ.
