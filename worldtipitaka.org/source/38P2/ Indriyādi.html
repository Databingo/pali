3491Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  upādinnā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
3492Paṭisandhikkhaṇe upādinnā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo; cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa…pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa…pe… . Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (2)
3493Upādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa…pe… . (Upādinnamūlake tīṇi, paṭhamasseva rūpajīvitindriyaṃ, itaresu natthi.) (3)
3494Anupādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  anupādinnā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
