417Sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo sahetukassa ceva na ca hetussa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
418Hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. (Indriyanti niyāmetabbaṃ, navapi paripuṇṇaṃ.)
419Sahetuko ceva na ca hetu dhammo sahetukassa ceva na ca hetussa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
420Hetu ceva sahetuko ca dhammo hetussa ceva sahetukassa ca dhammassa maggapaccayena paccayo…  sampayuttapaccayena paccayo…  atthipaccayena paccayo…  natthipaccayena paccayo…  vigatapaccayena paccayo…  avigatapaccayena paccayo.
