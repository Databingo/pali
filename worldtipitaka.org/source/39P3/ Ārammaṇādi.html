13.
20Hetu kusalo dhammo hetussa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
21Nahetu kusalo dhammo nahetussa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
22Hetu kusalo ca nahetu kusalo ca dhammā hetussa kusalassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
