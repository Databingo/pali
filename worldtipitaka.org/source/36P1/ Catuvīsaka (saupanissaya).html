Catuvīsaka (saupanissaya)
1783Noatthipaccayā nahetupaccayā naārammaṇapaccayā naadhipatipaccayā naanantarapaccayā nasamanantarapaccayā nasahajātapaccayā naaññamaññapaccayā nanissayapaccayā naupanissayapaccayā napurejātapaccayā napacchājātapaccayā naāsevanapaccayā navipākapaccayā naāhārapaccayā naindriyapaccayā najhānapaccayā namaggapaccayā nasampayuttapaccayā navippayuttapaccayā nonatthipaccayā novigatapaccayā noavigatapaccayā kamme dve.
