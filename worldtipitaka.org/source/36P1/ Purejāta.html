929Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ—  arahā cakkhuṃ aniccato dukkhato anattato vipassati. Sotaṃ…pe…  ghānaṃ…pe…  jivhaṃ…pe…  kāyaṃ…pe…  rūpe…pe…  sadde…pe…  gandhe…pe…  rase…pe…  phoṭṭhabbe…pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato dukkhato anattato vipassati, dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti.
