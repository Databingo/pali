964Kusalo dhammo kusalassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo—  kusalāni jhānaṅgāni sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ jhānapaccayena paccayo. (1)
965Kusalo dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo—  kusalāni jhānaṅgāni cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ jhānapaccayena paccayo. (2)
