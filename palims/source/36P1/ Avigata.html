20.
172Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kusalo dhammo uppajjati avigatapaccayā—  kusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā. (Avigatapaccayaṃ sahajātapaccayasadisaṃ.)
173(Ime tevīsatipaccayā sajjhāyantena vitthāretabbā.)

Avigata
302.
1004Kusalo dhammo kusalassa dhammassa avigatapaccayena paccayo—  kusalo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ avigatapaccayena paccayo. Tayo khandhā ekassa khandhassa avigatapaccayena paccayo. Dve khandhā dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ avigatapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
1005(Yathā atthipaccayaṃ, evaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)
1006Pañhāvārassa vibhaṅgo.
